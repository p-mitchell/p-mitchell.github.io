Undergratuate Degree

For my undergraduate Degree I studied Applied Psychology in IADT Dun Laoghaire.
I found this course really interesting, we studied a wide variety of topics such as Behavioural and Social psychology, which looked at how people interact with each other and within groups. We also studied more advanced topics like Neuropsychology and Cognitive Psychology, but the subjects I found most intetresting were the technical topics like Cyberpsychology and Web Application Development.

My passion for these subjects led me, as part of my final year project, to design and carry out an experiment, with ethical approval, and write a thesis based on my findings centered on how online anonymity effects our perception of online interactions.

I graduated from IADT in November of 2015 with a second class honours degree.
