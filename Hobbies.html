My Hobbies


My favourite sport is basketball. I first started playing during my first year of secondary school, so around 11 years ago.
I have had varying levels of success during my school career, with my team making it to at least the quarter finals of the South Dublin, Dublin, and All-Ireland competitions each year, but not winning a trophy since I was in second year.

I also play club basketball for UCD Marian Basketball Club.
I started playing for them when I was on the U16 and I am currently captain for the senior mens Division 2 team. Again I have had an up and down career, winning the league and cup double while I was a member of the U17's team, and finishing runner-up in the Division 1 cup last year.



Up until the start of this year, when I needed to focus more on my studies, I had been a volunteer basketball coach for the club as well.
I first started coaching as part of my Gaisce Award in transition year in secondary school, where I was an assistant coach with the u12's basketball team. After several years as an assistant coach I completed an introduction to coaches course and started coaching the new U11's team.
I really enjoyed coaching and helping out with the club and it is definitely something I would like to go back to after I am finished this course.



Another sport I am interested in is Snowboarding, I go away almost every winter to different resorts around France and Austria.
I am rather injury prone when it comes to snowloarding however, as I have torn ligaments in both my right and left knees one year apart, at the same resort, at almost identical places down the same run. So as a result my mother is refusing to go back to that place in france ever again.

Apart from sports I enjoy spending my spare time reading and sailing.
